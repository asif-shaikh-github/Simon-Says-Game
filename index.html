Simon Says Game

Press Start button to Play

How to Play:

  • Look for the "Start Game" button on the webpage.
  • Click the "Start Game" button to begin playing.
  • The game will show a sequence of colored buttons (yellow, green, purple, red).
  • Your goal is to repeat the same sequence by clicking on the buttons.
  • Each successful sequence advances you to the next level.
  • The level number is displayed at the top.
  • After pressing "Start Game," watch the colored buttons flash.
  • Click on the buttons in the same order to move to the next level.
  • Keep repeating the sequences correctly to achieve higher levels.
  • If you make a mistake, the game will end.
  • The final score (level reached) will be displayed in bold.
  • To play again, press the "Start Game" button after seeing the game over message.
  • Remember, the game gets faster as you progress, so stay sharp and have fun!
